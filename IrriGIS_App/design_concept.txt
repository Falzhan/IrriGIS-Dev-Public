Mobile App Design Concepts:

Global Theme & Styling
Background Color: Light Sage Green (approx. #E0EBE2)

Bottom Navigation/Footer Background: Grass Green (approx. #9BB88D)

Primary Accent (Buttons/Toggles): Muted Teal/Blue (approx. #74A5A8)

Card Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF)

Text Colors: Dark Charcoal/Black for primary text, Medium Gray for secondary text/timestamps.

Status Badges:
* "In Progress": Bright Mint Green text and background (approx. #2BD586)
* "Pending": Sky Blue text and background (approx. #5DADE2)

Border Radius: Highly rounded corners on cards, chips, and buttons (approx. 12px to 16px).

Camera Overlay: Translucent Black (rgba(0,0,0,0.6))

Rating Toggles (Unselected): Very pale lavender/blue border and text (approx. #E6E6FA)

Rating Toggles (Selected): Deep Lavender/Indigo background (approx. #7B61FF) with white text.

Text Input Background: White with a subtle gray border (#CCCCCC).

Camera Overlays: standard View with position="absolute".

Rating Scales (1-5): A custom component utilizing a flex-row of TouchableRipple or custom styled Button components to create the circular toggles.

Remarks Input: TextInput (with multiline={true}, mode="outlined").

Help Overlay (Quick Guide): Portal and Modal (or Dialog) components.

The Animation: use react-native-reanimated or React Native's built-in Animated API for the Bottom Tab Bar transition.



Component Mapping (React Native Paper)
Use these specific react-native-paper components:

Top Bar: Appbar.Header, Appbar.Action

Top Toggle Switch: SegmentedButtons

Status Pills/Badges: Chip (with customized heights and border radii)

Feed Items: Card, Card.Content (flex row layout)

Typography: Text (using variant="titleMedium", bodySmall, etc.)

Sidebar/Drawer: Drawer.Section, Drawer.Item, Avatar.Image

Dropdown/Collapsible Lists: List.AccordionGroup, List.Accordion, List.Item

!!!  II. Center: Camera icon
1. The Geo-Camera View & Navigation Animation

The Core Animation Logic:

Trigger:
* When the user clicks the center "Camera" icon on the standard green bottom navigation bar.

Action:
* The green background transitions to black.
* The center Camera icon physically translates (moves up on the Y-axis) and scales up to become the large, central Shutter button.

Replacement:
* As the camera icon rises, it leaves an empty space in the center of the navigation bar.
* That space fades in a new icon: a "Pencil/Form" icon.

State Result:
* The user is now in "Camera Mode".
* The bottom nav is black.
* Left is "Reports", Center is "Form" (inactive), Right is "Map".
* Above the center icon floats the giant white Shutter button.

Camera UI Overlays:

Top Bar:
* Absolute positioned translucent black row.
* Contains three white icons evenly spaced: Flash (lightning), Timer (clock), and Grid (hash).

Viewfinder:
* Full screen.
* Has a subtle white grid overlay (rule of thirds).

Geo-Tag Watermark:
* Absolute positioned at the bottom-left of the viewfinder, just above the shutter controls.
* It is a translucent dark box containing the Date, Time, Location Name, and precise GPS coordinates with a tiny crosshair icon.

Shutter Area:
* A black block at the bottom containing the floating white shutter button.
* To the right of the shutter button is a smaller camera-flip icon.



2. The Prompt Form (Bottom Sheet)

Trigger:
* Clicking the shutter button takes the photo, shrinks the camera view to the top third of the screen, and slides up a white form container from the bottom.

Header Row:
* Contains a "Presets" dropdown menu on the right, next to a circular "?" (Help) icon.

Rating Sections (Water Level, Silt Level, Debris Level):
* Each section has a bold title.
* Below the title is a flex-row of 5 circular buttons (labeled 1 through 5).

State:
* When unselected, circles have a thin purple border and purple text.
* When selected, the circle fills with solid purple and the text becomes white.

Labels:
* Directly beneath each number is a micro-copy label (e.g., under Water Level 1: "Dry", under 3: "Normal").

Remarks Section:
* Title: "Remarks".
* A large, outlined multiline TextInput area for user notes.

Submit Button:
* A floating, pill-shaped teal button that sits at the bottom center of the form, slightly overlapping the bottom navigation bar.

Bottom Navigation Bar (Form State):
* Reverts to the Grass Green background.
* The center "Pencil/Form" icon is now active, surrounded by a large blue pill-shaped highlight with the text "Form" below it.



3. The Quick Guide (Modal Overlay)

Trigger:
* Clicking the "?" icon in the form header.

Layout:
* A large white Modal that covers most of the screen, darkening/blurring the form behind it.
* Highly rounded corners.

Header:
* Bold "Quick Guide" title with a subtitle: "Select the rating based on your observation of the canal's state."

Data Matrix (The core content):
* A 4-column layout.
  * Column 1: The Number (1-5) inside a solid purple circle.
  * Column 2: Water Level description.
  * Column 3: Silt Level description.
  * Column 4: Debris Level description.

Example Row 1:
* (1) | Dry (No water) | Clean (No silt) | Clear (No obstruction).

This matrix uses light horizontal and vertical divider lines for readability.

Footer (Presets Guide):
* Title: "Presets"
* Explains automated preset groupings like "Cleaning" (documenting maintenance) and "Severe" (all parameters set to 5).
